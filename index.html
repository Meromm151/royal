About Us:-

El-Nasr Company for Services and Maintenance - Queen Service

It is one of the companies of the National Service Projects Organization, Ministry of Defense

  • It was established as an Egyptian Joint Stock Company in 1988 according to provisions of Law No. 159 of 1988 and its executive regulation.
  • It is one of the leading companies in the field of providing integrated services works inside and outside the Arab Republic of Egypt.
  • It applies modern management technology systems using market mechanisms based on ethical and professional standards through the application of mechanisms and methods of the successful private sector.
